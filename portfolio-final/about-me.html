About Me

I am a person that enjoys exploring new paths. I came across with digital media a few years ago and since then I can’t stop learning about the digital world. Animation is my best strength. The must important about it is that there is always something new to learn and that imagination has no limit. Creating animations in 3D is what I’m focusing right now, I want the world to see what is in my head. If I can contribute anything to the world, I hope that will be by entertaining people with my design skills. If I can help visualize a real world problem and the solution through graphics that’s an even better contribution, and that is something I’m looking for to start doing. Sometimes it can get difficult but that’s what I like about it, I love challenges.
